Akarahnet is a Minecraft server founded by, and named after, Endistic Akarah. Since its founding, the development team,
also known as the Pantheon, has formed, consisting of Endistic, Frost, Whirl, BladedEdge, Amaad, GlacialScythe, J22334455P,
Medley, MrXP, R J, and Seven. The server is genre-fluid, with aspects of factory games, grinding games, MMORPGs, and rage games.
